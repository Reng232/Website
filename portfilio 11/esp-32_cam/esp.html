Esp 32 Cam

This ESP-32 project showcases the utilization of advanced microcontroller technology to develop an innovative home security system. With features including motion detection and live video playback, it offers comprehensive surveillance capabilities to enhance home safety. Designed with a focus on reliability and functionality, this project represents a significant milestone in the team's portfolio, highlighting their commitment to delivering cutting-edge solutions for real-world applications.

Who We Are

Issac and Johnny spearhead this project, inspired by Jogita's innovative projects and their shared interest in home security. Motivated by a desire to make a difference in this field, they chose this project as a platform to apply their skills and expertise to address pressing issues in home security.

Key Features

Two pivotal features of this project are motion detection and live video playback. Motion detection technology enhances security by promptly identifying movement within the surveillance area, providing timely alerts. Additionally, the live video and playback features allow users to access and review recorded footage in real-time or retrospectively, ensuring comprehensive surveillance coverage and user convenience. These features underscore the project's commitment to delivering an effective and user-friendly home security solution.

Obstacles Faced

The project encountered obstacles such as retrieving the camera's IP address and addressing faulty connections. Obtaining the camera's IP address necessitated meticulous troubleshooting and configuration adjustments for seamless integration. Moreover, resolving faulty connections demanded thorough diagnostics and technical expertise to identify and rectify issues hindering system functionality. Despite these challenges, the team's commitment to problem-solving enabled them to navigate these hurdles and maintain progress towards their objectives.
