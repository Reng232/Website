Heart Rate Monitor

Explore an innovative project merging home security and medical technology. Inspired by renowned devices like the Ring DoorBell Camera, Valeria and Alex collaborated to develop a comprehensive home security system and heart rate monitor. Leveraging advanced microcontroller technology, the system offers real-time surveillance, while the heart rate monitor provides vital health insights. Reflecting their dedication to impactful solutions, this project embodies a fusion of modern technology and practicality.

Who We Are

Valeria and Alex's inspiration to create a heart rate monitor stems from their shared passion for the medical field. Alex, pursuing a career in sports medicine, is driven by a desire to assist athletes in maintaining optimal health and recovering from injuries more effectively. Meanwhile, Valeria aims to become a biomedical engineer, motivated by the potential to innovate and enhance medical devices that improve patient care. Together, they identified the heart rate monitor as the perfect project to merge their interests, allowing them to apply their knowledge and skills to develop a tool that promotes health and well-being.

Key Features

One key feature of this project is its ability to measure beats per minute (BPM), providing users with real-time information about their heart rate. This functionality enables individuals to monitor their cardiovascular health and track changes in their heart rate over time. By incorporating BPM monitoring, the project enhances its utility as a comprehensive heart rate monitoring solution for users seeking to maintain optimal health and well-being.

Materials

The materials that were used in this project were the following:

  • 3d printed clip
  • pulse sensor
  • male to male wires
  • esp 32
  • electrical tape

Obstacles Faced

Facing unexpected setbacks, the project shifted from the ESP32 to the Raspberry Pi Model 5 after damage occurred to the laptop, rendering its ports unusable. Moreover, a critical component, the pulse sensor, broke during this transition. Despite these challenges, the team remained resilient, adapting their approach to overcome setbacks and continue progressing towards their goals.
