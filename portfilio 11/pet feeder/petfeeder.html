Pet feeder

Introducing a cutting-edge, remotely controlled food and water dispenser for pets, powered by advanced ESP32 technology. This sleek device, featuring a sophisticated design and real-time monitoring, ensures your pets are always well-fed and hydrated. Discover more about this innovative solution as you scroll

Who We Are

Inspired by their previous work assisting individuals with disabilities, Adam and Olivia embarked on a mission to simplify pet care and reduce stress for pet owners. Drawing from personal experience, they envisioned a device that could be controlled via phone to effortlessly feed pets. Their ultimate goal is to alleviate everyday worries and improve the quality of life for pet owners.

Key Features

Two key features of the pet feeder are its ESP32-provided Wi-Fi connectivity and the ability to control it through a website. This allows pet owners to manage feeding schedules remotely and conveniently. The user-friendly interface ensures easy monitoring and adjustment from anywhere. Combining advanced technology with practical functionality, this feeder ensures pets are always well-fed and cared for.

Materials

The materials that were used in this project were the following:

  • Esp 32
  • relay
  • water pump
  • load cell
  • ultra sonic sensor
  • diode
  • cnc shield
  • stepper motor
  • voltage regulator
  • 3d printed parts

Obstacles Faced

Some obstacles Adam and Olivia faced included their water pump emitting strong EMF,
which caused the stepper motor to fidget uncontrollably.
Another challenge arose when powering the object.
This made it difficult for Adam and Olivia to maintain stability and control.
Finding solutions to these issues became imperative for their project's success.
