Key Features

This project comprises two essential components: the body and the transmission. The body forms the structural foundation, offering stability and support, while the transmission facilitates power transfer for smooth operation. These key features underscore the project's commitment to both reliability and functionality, reflecting a dedication to quality design and engineering.

Materials

The materials that were used in this project were the following:

  • transmissions
  • 3d printed parts
  • 12v battery
  • circuit breaker
  • Wheels

Obstacles Faced

The project encountered a few obstacles, including difficulties with Onshape due to unfamiliarity and the loss of access. Additionally, there were challenges with excess wiring in the power distribution board and faulty wiring in the transmission. Despite these setbacks, the team persevered, addressing each challenge with determination and problem-solving skills to ensure the project's success.
