Clash of the Superheros

"Clash of the Superheroes" stands as a prototype fighting game crafted utilizing Python and PyGame, conceptualized within the framework of a school project. Within this game, players are presented with a selection of three distinct characters, each endowed with their own set of unique special attacks and individual attributes.

Who We Are

Jennifer and Jose, the creative minds behind the project, drew inspiration from their shared passion for gaming and digital art. Influenced by beloved titles such as Mortal Kombat, X-Men, and Children of the Atom, they embarked on a mission to blend their love for these games with their artistic prowess. Their project not only pays homage to these iconic titles but also serves as a platform to explore new realms of creativity and innovation. With their combined expertise and unwavering dedication, Jennifer and Jose are committed to pushing the boundaries of artistic expression in their endeavor.

Key Features

Two pivotal features distinguish this project: firstly, the characters showcase distinct special abilities tailored to their individual traits, enhancing gameplay diversity; secondly, meticulous attention to character design ensures a visually captivating experience. Additionally, players can immerse themselves in varied environments with the option to select between two distinct maps: the nostalgic ambience of Waffle House and the futuristic allure of the Cyber Sci-Fi map.

Obstacles Faced

Throughout the project, the team faced various obstacles, notably in the areas of character posing and collision coding.
Achieving visual consistency proved challenging as the team worked to proportion character poses accurately.
Additionally, coding character collisions required intricate programming to ensure realistic interactions within the game environment.
Despite these challenges, the team's dedication and perseverance enabled them to overcome obstacles and deliver a polished final product that met their standards of excellence.
